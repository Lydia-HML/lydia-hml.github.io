關於我

About Me

你好！我是林筱玫 (Lydia Hsiao-Mei Lin)，目前在國立台灣科技大學企業管理系擔任專案助理教授，致力於人工智慧、物聯網、設計思考和創新創業領域的研究與教學。

Hello! I'm Hsiao-Mei Lin, currently serving as a Project Assistant Professor in the Department of Business Administration at National Taiwan University of Science and Technology, dedicated to research and teaching in AI, IoT, Design Thinking, and Innovation & Entrepreneurship.

我擁有豐富的產學經驗，曾任創業公司創辦人與執行長，並在多個公共事務與社會影響力領域擔任重要角色，如台灣人工智慧協會常務理事兼執行長、台灣女科技人學會理事等。

I have extensive industry-academia experience, having served as a founder and CEO of a startup, and in various public affairs and social impact roles, such as Executive Director of the Taiwan Artificial Intelligence Association and Director of the Taiwan Women in Science and Technology Association.
